1. 修改配置文件

`vim /etc/ssh/sshd_config`

去除`#PermitRootLogin prohibit-password`的`#`，并且将`prohibit-password`改为`yes`
去除`#PasswordAuthentication no`的`#`，并且将`no`改为`yes`

2.重启服务

`service sshd restart`
